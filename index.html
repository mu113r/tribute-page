1955 - Present

Emerico Muller, aka Professor Muller (XUXU), was born in São Paulo, São Paulo, Brazil in 1955. Graduated in Biology in 1977.

He is one of the best teachers to prepare students for the "vestibular" test (this is an exam brasiliens have to take to be able to enter a university). The economic conditions on his country made him diversify his knowledge and now he is teaching 4 subjects (Math, Physics, Biology and Chemestry).

He also has worked for the police department doing crime scene investigation and reports that helped investigators to find whos blame was for the unfotunate accident that killed or injuried someone.
